## Ingredients

### For the Eggplant Layer
- 2 large eggplants, sliced 1/4 inch thick
- Olive oil for brushing
- Salt

### For the Meat Sauce
- 1 lb ground lamb (or beef)
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1/2 cup red wine
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp oregano
- 1 bay leaf
- Salt and pepper

### For the BÃ©chamel
- 4 tbsp butter
- 4 tbsp flour
- 2 cups milk, warmed
- 1/4 tsp nutmeg
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Salt eggplant slices and let sit for 30 minutes, then rinse and pat dry.
2. Brush eggplant with olive oil and roast at 400Â°F for 20 minutes until tender. ![ğŸ†+ğŸ”¥](emojikitchen/1f525_1f60a.png)
3. For meat sauce: Brown ground lamb in a large pan, breaking it up.
4. Add onion and garlic, cook until softened.
5. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, wine, cinnamon, oregano, and bay leaf. ![ğŸ§„+ğŸ”¥](emojikitchen/1f9c4_1f525.png)
6. Simmer for 30 minutes until thick. Season with salt and pepper.
7. For bÃ©chamel: Melt butter, whisk in flour, cook for 2 minutes.
8. Gradually add warm milk, whisking constantly until thickened.
9. Remove from heat, stir in nutmeg, egg yolks, and half the Parmesan.
10. To assemble: Layer half the eggplant in a 9x13 baking dish.
