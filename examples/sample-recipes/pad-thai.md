# Pad Thai

Classic Thai street food with rice noodles and shrimp.

## Ingredients

- 8 oz rice noodles
- 8 large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups bean sprouts
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 tablespoons fish sauce
- 2 tablespoons tamarind paste
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/4 cup roasted peanuts, crushed
- 2 green onions, sliced
- Lime wedges for serving

## Instructions

1. Soak rice noodles in warm water for 30 minutes until softened. Drain and set aside.
2. Heat oil in a wok over high heat. Add garlic and stir fry until fragrant.
3. Add shrimp and cook until pink, about 2-3 minutes.
4. Push ingredients to the side, crack eggs into the wok and scramble.
5. Add noodles, fish sauce, tamarind paste, and sugar. Toss everything together.
6. Add bean sprouts and green onions, stir fry for 1-2 minutes.
7. Remove from heat and top with crushed peanuts.

## Serving Suggestions

Serve hot with lime wedges on the side. Garnish with extra peanuts and cilantro if desired.
