# Moussaka

Greek layered eggplant casserole with spiced meat and béchamel sauce.

## Ingredients

### For the Eggplant Layer
- 2 large eggplants, sliced 1/4 inch thick
- Olive oil for brushing
- Salt

### For the Meat Sauce
- 1 lb ground lamb (or beef)
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1/2 cup red wine
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp oregano
- 1 bay leaf
- Salt and pepper

### For the Béchamel
- 4 tbsp butter
- 4 tbsp flour
- 2 cups milk, warmed
- 1/4 tsp nutmeg
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Salt eggplant slices and let sit for 30 minutes, then rinse and pat dry.
2. Brush eggplant with olive oil and roast at 400°F for 20 minutes until tender.
3. For meat sauce: Brown ground lamb in a large pan, breaking it up.
4. Add onion and garlic, cook until softened.
5. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, wine, cinnamon, oregano, and bay leaf.
6. Simmer for 30 minutes until thick. Season with salt and pepper.
7. For béchamel: Melt butter, whisk in flour, cook for 2 minutes.
8. Gradually add warm milk, whisking constantly until thickened.
9. Remove from heat, stir in nutmeg, egg yolks, and half the Parmesan.
10. To assemble: Layer half the eggplant in a 9x13 baking dish.
11. Top with all the meat sauce, then remaining eggplant.
12. Pour béchamel over top, sprinkle with remaining Parmesan.
13. Bake at 350°F for 45 minutes until golden and bubbling.
14. Let rest 15 minutes before serving.

## Notes

Moussaka is often made ahead and reheated. The flavors improve overnight. Traditionally served with a simple Greek salad.
