# Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

Creamy North Indian curry with tender chicken in tomato-butter sauce.

## Ingredients

### For the Marinade
- 1 lb boneless chicken thighs, cubed
- 1/2 cup yogurt
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp chili powder
- Salt to taste

### For the Sauce
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 2 tbsp honey
- Fresh cilantro for garnish

## Instructions

1. Mix chicken with marinade ingredients and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
2. Heat butter in a large pan over medium heat.
3. Add onions and cook until golden, about 8 minutes.
4. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
5. Stir in garam masala, cumin, and coriander. Toast for 30 seconds.
6. Add crushed tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes.
7. Blend the sauce until smooth (optional, for a silkier texture).
8. Return sauce to pan, add marinated chicken.
9. Cook for 15-20 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
10. Stir in cream and honey, simmer for 5 minutes.
11. Garnish with cilantro and serve with naan or basmati rice.

## Tips

For extra richness, add a tablespoon of butter (makhan) just before serving. Some versions include kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves) for authentic flavor.
