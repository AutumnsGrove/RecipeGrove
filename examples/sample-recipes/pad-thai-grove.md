## Ingredients

- 8 oz rice noodles
- 8 large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups bean sprouts
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 tablespoons fish sauce
- 2 tablespoons tamarind paste
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/4 cup roasted peanuts, crushed ![ğŸ¥œ+ğŸ˜Š](emojikitchen/1f95c_1f60a.png)
- 2 green onions, sliced
- Lime wedges for serving

## Instructions

1. Soak rice noodles in warm water for 30 minutes until softened. Drain and set aside.
2. Heat oil in a wok over high heat. Add garlic and stir fry until fragrant. ![ğŸ§„+ğŸ”¥](emojikitchen/1f9c4_1f525.png)
3. Add shrimp and cook until pink, about 2-3 minutes.
4. Push ingredients to the side, crack eggs into the wok and scramble. ![ğŸ¥š+ğŸ”¥](emojikitchen/1f95a_1f525.png)
5. Add noodles, fish sauce, tamarind paste, and sugar. Toss everything together.
6. Add bean sprouts and green onions, stir fry for 1-2 minutes. ![ğŸŒ±+ğŸ˜Š](emojikitchen/1f331_1f60a.png)
7. Remove from heat and top with crushed peanuts.

Serve hot with lime wedges on the side. Garnish with extra peanuts and cilantro if desired.
