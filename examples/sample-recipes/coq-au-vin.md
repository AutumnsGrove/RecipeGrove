# Coq au Vin

Classic French braised chicken in red wine sauce.

## Ingredients

- 1 whole chicken (3-4 lbs), cut into 8 pieces
- 6 oz bacon, diced
- 2 cups red wine (Burgundy or Pinot Noir)
- 1 cup chicken stock
- 2 tbsp brandy or cognac
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 carrots, cut into chunks
- 1 onion, quartered
- 8 oz pearl onions, peeled
- 8 oz mushrooms, halved
- 2 bay leaves
- 4 sprigs fresh thyme
- 2 tbsp flour
- 2 tbsp butter
- Salt and pepper
- Fresh parsley for garnish

## Instructions

1. Season chicken pieces generously with salt and pepper.
2. In a large Dutch oven, cook bacon until crispy. Remove and set aside.
3. Brown chicken in bacon fat on all sides, working in batches. Set aside.
4. Add carrots and quartered onion to pot, cook for 5 minutes.
5. Add garlic, cook for 1 minute.
6. Pour in brandy and carefully flame it (or let alcohol cook off).
7. Add wine, stock, bay leaves, and thyme. Bring to a simmer.
8. Return chicken and bacon to pot, cover and simmer for 45 minutes.
9. In a separate pan, saut√© pearl onions and mushrooms in butter until golden.
10. Add pearl onions and mushrooms to the pot, cook for 15 more minutes.
11. Remove chicken and vegetables. Reduce sauce until thickened.
12. Whisk in butter to finish sauce.
13. Return everything to pot, adjust seasoning.
14. Garnish with parsley and serve with crusty bread or mashed potatoes.

## Serving Suggestions

This dish is even better the next day as flavors meld. Traditionally served with boiled potatoes or buttered egg noodles.
